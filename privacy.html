Privacy Draft

Adria Fusion Sailing uses browser geolocation only after the user presses the GPS button and grants permission. The prototype does not send location data to an Adria Fusion backend.

  • Live weather requests are sent directly from the browser to Open-Meteo APIs with the selected latitude and longitude.
  • The app stores cached app files and forecast responses locally through the browser cache/service worker.
  • No account, payment, advertising identifier, analytics SDK or push notification is implemented in this build.
  • For App Store or Google Play release, this draft must be reviewed and hosted at a stable public URL.

This app is not a nautical chart, official maritime warning service or navigation instrument.
