Data Sources

The app shows source health and confidence caps because marine forecasts near a coast can be materially wrong.

  • Open-Meteo Weather API: temperature, wind, precipitation probability.
  • Open-Meteo Marine API: wave height, swell/wind wave fields, sea surface temperature, ocean current model values.
  • DHMZ XML/CAP seed: Croatian observation and warning context from the latest local adapter snapshot.
  • OpenStreetMap Standard Tiles: live base map for prototype positioning and coastline context.
  • Esri World Imagery: real-map satellite base layer for visual coastline and harbor context.
  • OpenSeaMap seamark tiles: visual seamark context for harbor and navigation-aid awareness.
  • EMODnet Bathymetry OGC WMS: mean-depth colour, contour and quality-index overlays for European sea depth context.
  • GEBCO WMS: global shaded bathymetry relief context.
  • Device forecast archive: browser-local snapshots from live forecast loads, retained for future verification weighting.
  • HHI/authorized ENC distributors: official Croatian chart data is required for navigation-grade product claims and is not bundled in this prototype.
  • Copernicus Marine, ECMWF and MET Norway are planned as additional independent providers before claiming high-confidence fusion.

Navigation limits

Depth, contour, seamark and forecast overlays are planning context only. They are not an official nautical chart and must not be used as the sole basis for safety at sea.

Confidence

Confidence is capped when independent live sources or local truth are missing. The app must show small/limited layers rather than precise-looking visuals when the data basis is weak.
