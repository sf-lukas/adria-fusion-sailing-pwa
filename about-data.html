Data Sources

The app shows source health and confidence caps because marine forecasts near a coast can be materially wrong.

  • Open-Meteo Weather API: temperature, wind, precipitation probability.
  • Open-Meteo Marine API: wave height, swell/wind wave fields, sea surface temperature, ocean current model values.
  • DHMZ XML/CAP seed: Croatian observation and warning context from the latest local adapter snapshot.
  • Copernicus Marine, ECMWF and MET Norway are planned as additional independent providers before claiming high-confidence fusion.

Confidence

Confidence is capped when independent live sources or local truth are missing. The app must show small/limited layers rather than precise-looking visuals when the data basis is weak.
